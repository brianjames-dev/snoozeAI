# Home Screen Data Viz Ideas (SnoozeAI)

1) Daily/Weekly Snoozes Over Time  
   - Line or area chart showing count of snoozed items per day/week.  
   - Highlights adoption and spikes on specific days.

2) Resurfacing Schedule Density  
   - Histogram/bar chart of snoozes by upcoming hour/time-of-day.  
   - Shows when notifications will return; indicates clustering in “Always on” mode.

3) Urgency Distribution Over Time  
   - Stacked bars by day/week: low/medium/high counts.  
   - Reveals whether you’re snoozing more urgent vs low-priority items over time.

4) Source/Category Breakdown  
   - Pie/donut or horizontal bar: top apps/categories (via package name/hints).  
   - Insight into what you snooze most (e.g., Slack vs email vs alerts).

5) Average Snooze Duration  
   - Line/area: average (and 90th percentile) snooze length per day/week.  
   - Shows trend toward shorter vs longer snoozes.

6) Resurfacing Outcomes (if tracked)  
   - Bar chart: resurfaced and actioned vs re-snoozed vs dismissed.  
   - Evaluates effectiveness of snoozes.

7) Quiet Window vs Actual Resurfacing  
   - Overlay line/area: resurfaces inside vs outside quiet window.  
   - Indicates if “Always on” smart resurfacing respects intent.

8) Backend Health / Latency (optional)  
   - Sparkline of summarize/classify/store response times.  
   - Useful for troubleshooting slowdowns.
